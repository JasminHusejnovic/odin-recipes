Chicken and Potatoes

Description

This is your most basic recipe for introductory cooks. You just clean a chicken, season it, bake it, put some potatoes in, bake some more, garnish and voila.

Incrediants

  • Chicken
  • Any type of potato
  • Salt, pepper, and whatever seasoning you may like
  • Parsley for garnish
  • Side salad of your choice

Steps

  1. Take your chicken out and clean it by taking out any feathers in the skin and giving it a rinse. Then take out the parts inside the chicken that you won't eat.
  2. Preheat your oven at 350
  3. While it's heating up, season your chicken all over. You can also use a rub.
  4. Place the chicken in a pan with a light amount of oil and put it in the oven for the next 30 minutes
  5. While it's baking, peel your potatoes if you need to, or season them as well.
  6. Take out the chicken and place the poatoes in the same pan as the chicken and put it back in the oven
  7. While they're baking, make a side salad of whatever you like.
  8. After 1 hour, take out the chicken and garnish it with the parsley.
